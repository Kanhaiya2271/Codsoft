Who We Are

PawFinder started in 2025 as a small group of animal lovers who wanted to make adoption easier and more accessible for everyone. What began in a tiny garage with just 5 rescued cats has now grown into a community-driven platform that has helped over 3,000+ pets find their forever homes.

We work closely with local shelters, foster families and veterinary clinics to make sure every animal gets the love and care they need before and after adoption.
